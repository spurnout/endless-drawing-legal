Privacy Policy — Endless Drawing

iRolled20, LLC · Effective June 6, 2026

Endless Drawing (com.irolled20.endlessdrawing) is a local-first infinite-canvas drawing app. We designed it to keep your work on your device. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles, why, and the limited cases where data leaves your device — always at your initiative.

Summary

  • Your drawings stay on your device by default. We run no servers and do not collect, transmit, or sell your artwork or personal information.
  • No analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • Two optional features can move data off-device, only when you turn them on: Google Drive backup and screen recording.

Data the app stores on your device

  • Drawings and documents — strokes, imported images, layers, bookmarks, palettes, themes, and per-canvas settings.
  • App preferences — brush, color, and display settings.

This data lives in the app's private storage and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Android Auto Backup

If you have Android system backup enabled, Android may copy the app's drawings and preferences to your personal Google account backup — a standard Android feature controlled by your device settings. We explicitly exclude your connected-account identity (see below) from this backup.

Google Drive backup (optional)

If you choose to connect a Google account for backup:

  • We use Sign in with Google and request a single, narrow scope — drive.file. This grants access only to files the app itself creates in your Drive. It does not give access to your other Drive files.
  • Backups contain your own drawings, uploaded to your own Google Drive. We never receive or store a copy on any server of ours.
  • When connected, the app displays and stores on your device the connected account's email and display name so you can see which account is linked. This identity is excluded from cloud backup and device transfer.
  • Access tokens are held in memory only during a backup and are never written to disk or logged.
  • You can disconnect the account at any time in the app, clearing the stored identity and any in-memory token.

Your use of Google Drive is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Screen recording (optional)

  • Recording only starts when you explicitly tap record and grant the Android screen-capture permission. A persistent notification is shown while recording.
  • Recordings are saved locally to Movies/Endless Drawing. They are not uploaded or transmitted by the app.

Permissions we request and why

  • Internet / Network state — only to upload to your Google Drive when you use the optional backup feature. No network calls are made otherwise.
  • Foreground service / media projection — to run the optional screen recorder.
  • Post notifications — to show the recording-in-progress notification (with a Stop control) on Android 13 and later.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or artwork with third parties. The only data transfer is the optional, user-initiated upload of your own drawings to your own Google Drive.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Data retention and deletion

All app data is stored locally. To delete it, clear the app's data or uninstall the app. To delete Drive backups, remove the app's files from your Google Drive, or disconnect the account in-app and delete the backup folder it created.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this document.
